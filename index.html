Who Am I?

I thrive on finding creative solutions to problems. I have developed skills to consistently produce a desired result, quickly, effectively and within production or budgetary constraints. Transitioning in to training I have honed my ability to understand the needs of an individual, or a group and provide a satisfying and empowering learning experience. Empowering and inspiring others, I continually learn and develop my own skills. I have an extensive knowledge of technology and software, with particular expertise in the Creative field and Apple technology. I am certified in all Apple Pro software. In my personal life I am keen aero-modeller, music fan. I am improving my skills in video and audio production, refreshing my skills in HTML, CSS and JavaScript as well as beginning to learn Swift.

What do I do?

Falling Over

Some of these I had made entirely from scratch.
